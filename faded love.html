Faded Love

I think that the image of each other in the eyes of lovers is never the same, and they seldom see each other's image objectively. In love, the relationship between lovers will go through many stages. In each stage, the relationship between lovers' interaction and feelings will be different, and the image in each other's eyes will also change accordingly.
The image of a lover is not always the same, it is an image controlled by time, events, emotions, etc. It is no longer the objective and real appearance of a lover, but perhaps a lover who is composed of many aspects is the real appearance of a lover. Therefore, in the center of the whole exhibition, the image of the lover is divided into four periods. From back to front, they are the starting period, the hot-blooded period, the teething period, and the stable period. The images of lovers in the four periods are combined together to show the mixed memories of lovers in the human brain.
